Ivan Svogor, Ph.D.

Trained as a Software Engineer, but passionate for Embedded Systems and Robotics. Always seeking more, he pivoted towards Robotics as external research associate at Malardalen University in Sweden, receiving a Ph.D. From University of Zagreb in Croatia. He specialized in Software Architecture for Swarm Robotics through postdoctoral fellowship at Montreal Polytechnique. At the moment, he is working in public sector (University), but also engages in interesting freelance projects; providing consulting services and talks.
